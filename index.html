Population Diversity Geo Data Visualizer

This is a Geo Visualization of Diversity in the US. The choropleth map of the US represents the diversityIndex which is a measure of how diverse a state is. Diversity Index is generally high where most communities have good representation. If there is one clear majority and negligible minority population, diversity index is low. The lighter the shade, the lower the diversity index and vice versa.

If you click on a particular state, you will zoom into the state and get a bar graph displaying the population difference of different communities. When you click again, you will zoom out of the bar graph. You can hold and move the graph and zoom in or zoom out on the graph view port. I am compiling counties data to calculate the shade of the state in the choropleth.For extra credit, while hovering over the states, you would be able to recognize their diversity Index along with the State Name on the existing Choropleth representation.
